Note.OS

Note.OS is a user-friendly note-taking platform designed to help you organize your thoughts, ideas, and tasks seamlessly. With Note.OS, you can create, edit, and store your notes effortlessly, ensuring that your important information is always within your reach!

It comes with many features:

Customization

Note.OS offers a lot of customization settings, from every color in the website down to the background images.

Calendar

A Calender inside the website to conveniently keep track of all your tasks every day of the month, every month of the year.

Video Player

A built-in video player, so you can watch videos if you need a break from writing or you can listen to music / audio books in the background.

Photo Album

A photo album where you can save images that you can go back to at a later time.
